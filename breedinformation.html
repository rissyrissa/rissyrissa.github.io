History & Origin

Cavapoos are a delightful crossbreed that combines the best traits of Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Poodles. Let's explore their background:

What Are Cavapoos?

Originally from the 20th century, Cavapoos are believed to be first bred in the US or Australia during the 1990s as designer dogs that combine the intelligence of Poodles with the affectionate nature of Cavaliers.

Parent Breeds

Crossbreeds of both Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Poodles (typically Miniature or Toy varieties). Cavaliers are a small-sized companion breed from the UK, part of the Spaniel group, known for their gentle temperament.

Height 9 to 14 inches
Weight 9 to 25 lbs
Lifespan 12 to 15 years

Varieties of Cavapoos

The size of a Cavapoo depends on the Poodle parent, ranging from Toy to Miniature sizes. Here are the common varieties:

Toy Poodle Mix

  • Weight: 8-15 lbs
  • Height: 9-11 inches
  • Best for: Apartment living

The smallest variety, perfect for those who want a tiny companion.

Miniature Poodle Mix

  • Weight: 15-25 lbs
  • Height: 12-14 inches
  • Best for: Families with children

The most common size, offering a great balance between small size and sturdiness.

Standard Poodle Mix

  • Weight: 25-40 lbs
  • Height: 15+ inches
  • Best for: Active households

Less common but great for those who want a larger, more active companion.

Personality & Temperament

Friendly

Cavapoos are known for their affectionate and people-oriented nature.

Intelligent

Inheriting the Poodle's smarts, they're quick learners and eager to please.

Great with Kids

Gentle and patient, making them excellent family pets.

Adaptable

Comfortable in both apartments and houses with yards.
