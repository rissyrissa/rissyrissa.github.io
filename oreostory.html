Time to learn about who inspired this site to begin with!
Scroll down to learn all about my dog, Oreo

March 2020

We came across Oreo (back then he was Echo)
on the Lancaster website. The breeders were
mennonite farmers in PA; not only was the house
clean when we got there, but little Echo was playing
with his siblings comfortably. We made a few visits to see
him, with each visit asking the breeders more questions

May 2020

On the 15th, my Mom and I made one last trip,
only this time it was to pick Echo up. And boy was
fun! Most puppies are full of energy, but this one
so calm and just curious about the world. When we
brought him home, we renamed Echo to Oreo!!

July 2021

At this point, Oreo's fur is fully grown and
he's potty-trained! His calm personality is more
unhinged since he's now used to the family and
our overall dynamic. In the photo, you can see
chilling outside during the 4th of July

August 2022

Now 2 years old, Oreo has gotten bigger and
more bold with his choices. He's gotten the idea
that he could drive as shown here and also became
more rebellious around the house. This called for
more training, which made him remember the rules
of the household itself.

November 2023

With me off to school, Oreo had to learn the hard way
that I was no longer going to be in the house often.
Mom reported a some anxiety and even depression in
Oreo's behavior. Which is why she and Jacob made it
their mission to spend lots of time with him! He's
visited to Mount many times, however he's given
quality care and time at home and now he's much
better!

Present Day

Nowadays, Oreo is living his best life back at home.
Sure he's still the same chill puppy he was before
But now as a 5 year old Cavapoo, he's proven to not
only be the favorite in the family, but a great
reminder that cavapoos are by far the best breeds
there is!
